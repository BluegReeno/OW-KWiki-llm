# Ignitis Renewables

Ignitis Renewables is the renewable energy development arm of Lithuania's Ignitis Group. In
2023, a joint venture between Ignitis Renewables and Ocean Winds won development rights for a
700 MW site in Lithuania's first offshore wind auction, becoming the developer of the
[Curonian Nord](/projects/curonian-nord.md) offshore wind farm in the Baltic Sea. In 2025, the
partners signed a sale and purchase agreement under which Ignitis acquired full ownership of the
project.

In July 2026, Ignitis received a positive Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) decision from
Lithuania's Environmental Protection Agency for Curonian Nord, clearing the project to proceed
to its next permitting stage.

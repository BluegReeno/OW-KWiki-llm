# JERA Nex BP

JERA Nex BP is an offshore wind joint venture between Japan's JERA and bp. In Belgium it develops, builds, and operates offshore wind farms from an operations hub in Ostend, including [Northwester 2](/projects/northwester-2.md) (now wholly owned) and [Nobelwind](/projects/nobelwind.md) (80.1% owned). It is also active in UK, Australian, and Taiwanese offshore wind development (e.g. Mona, Morven, Blue Mackerel).

## Key Transactions
- **July 2026**: Completed acquisition of [Sumitomo Corporation](#)'s remaining stakes in Northwester 2 (30%) and Nobelwind (39.02%), taking full ownership of Northwester 2 and raising its Nobelwind stake to 80.1%.

# Companies

* [Ørsted](orsted.md) - Danish energy company that transformed from state oil-and-gas group DONG Energy into the world's largest offshore wind developer, with major projects across the UK, Continental Europe, Taiwan, and the US.
* [Equinor](equinor.md) - Norwegian, majority state-owned energy company (formerly Statoil) and a leading offshore wind developer, pioneering floating wind (Hywind) alongside large fixed-bottom projects such as Dogger Bank (UK) and Empire Wind (US).
* [RWE](rwe-offshore-wind.md) - German multinational utility and the world's second-largest offshore wind operator, developing, building, and operating wind farms across the UK, Germany, Denmark, the Netherlands, and other markets.
* [Iberdrola / Avangrid](iberdrola.md) - Spanish multinational utility and one of the world's largest offshore wind developers, active across Europe, the US (via wholly-owned subsidiary Avangrid) and Asia-Pacific.
* [Vestas Wind Systems A/S](vestas.md) - Danish wind turbine manufacturer, the world's largest by installed capacity, supplying both onshore and offshore turbines including the V236-15.0 MW offshore platform.
* [Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy](siemens-gamesa.md) - Wind turbine manufacturer headquartered in Spain, wholly owned by Siemens Energy since 2023, and one of the leading suppliers of offshore direct-drive turbines worldwide.
* [GE Vernova](ge-vernova-offshore-wind.md) - American energy equipment maker and offshore wind turbine OEM, spun off from General Electric in 2024, best known for its Haliade-X offshore turbine platform.
* [DEME Offshore](deme-offshore.md) - Belgian marine engineering and offshore installation contractor, part of DEME Group, providing EPCI services (foundations, cables, substations) for offshore wind farms worldwide.
* [Van Oord](van-oord.md) - Dutch family-owned marine contractor specializing in dredging, marine engineering, and offshore wind installation (foundations, cables) across Europe, Asia, and the US.
* [Vattenfall](vattenfall.md) - Swedish state-owned multinational utility and offshore wind developer, active in the UK, Netherlands, Denmark, and Germany, including the 1.6 GW Nordlicht cluster.
* [Skyborn Renewables](skyborn.md) - GIP-backed offshore wind developer, sole owner of Germany's Nordergründe offshore wind farm and JV partner with Ørsted on US projects Revolution Wind and South Fork Wind.
* [Føn Energy Services](fon-energy-services.md) - Renewable energy O&M service provider; Polish arm holds a multi-year O&M contract for the Baltic Power offshore wind farm.
* [Kent](kent.md) - Engineering and technical consulting firm; appointed Owner's Engineer for the Buchan floating wind project in Scotland, July 2026.
* [Aker Solutions](aker-solutions.md) - Norwegian engineering company delivering EPC steel substructures for HVDC offshore converter platforms, including East Anglia Three, Norfolk Vanguard, and Germany's BalWin1/BalWin2 grid connections.
* [LS Cable & System / LS GreenLink](ls-cable-system.md) - South Korean cable manufacturer building a subsea power cable factory in the US via subsidiary LS GreenLink, to supply HVDC/HVAC export cables for offshore wind and grid interconnection.
* [Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP)](copenhagen-infrastructure-partners.md) - Danish fund manager and offshore wind developer/investor active in Europe, the US, and Asia-Pacific; secured 1 GW across two projects in South Korea's 2026 offshore wind auction.
* [ESB](esb.md) - Irish state-owned energy group; 50:50 JV partner (with Red Rock Renewables) on Scotland's Inch Cape offshore wind farm, and JV partner (with EDF Power Solutions) on the Gwynt Glas floating wind project in the Celtic Sea.
* [JERA Nex BP](jera-nex-bp.md) - Offshore wind joint venture between JERA and bp, operating a hub in Ostend, Belgium; took full ownership of Northwester 2 and raised its Nobelwind stake to 80.1% by buying out Sumitomo Corporation, July 2026.
* [Bernhard Schulte Offshore (BSO)](bernhard-schulte-offshore.md) - Hamburg-based offshore wind service vessel operator running a fleet of Ulstein-designed WINDEA-branded CSOVs; completed a four-vessel CSOV newbuild series with the delivery of Windea Clarke, July 2026.
* [Ignitis Renewables](ignitis-renewables.md) - Lithuanian state-controlled energy group's renewable arm; sole developer of the 700 MW Curonian Nord offshore wind farm, Lithuania's first offshore wind project.

# ESB

ESB (Electricity Supply Board) is an Irish state-owned energy group with a
growing offshore wind development portfolio in the UK and Ireland.

## Key Projects

- **[Inch Cape Offshore Wind Farm](/projects/inch-cape.md)** - 1.1 GW,
  Scotland, North Sea. 50:50 joint venture with Red Rock Renewables
  (Inch Cape Offshore Limited), formed November 2020. Under construction,
  first power expected late 2026.
- **[Gwynt Glas Floating Wind Project](/projects/gwynt-glas.md)** - up to
  1.5 GW proposed floating wind farm in the Celtic Sea, Wales, developed
  jointly with EDF Power Solutions, with DP Energy as exclusive development
  partner. EIA Scoping Report submitted July 2026.

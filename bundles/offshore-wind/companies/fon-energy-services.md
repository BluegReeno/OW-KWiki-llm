# Føn Energy Services

Føn Energy Services Poland is the Polish arm of Føn Energy Services, a renewable energy
operations & maintenance (O&M) provider. In July 2026 it signed a long-term, multi-million-euro
O&M agreement with the joint venture behind [Baltic Power](/projects/baltic-power.md), Poland's
first offshore wind farm, developed by ORLEN and Northland Power.

# Overview

Aker Solutions is a Norwegian engineering company supplying steel
substructures and EPC (engineering, procurement, construction) work for
offshore [HVDC grid connection](/technology/hvdc-grid-connection.md)
platforms. It has become a repeat supplier on European offshore wind grid
connections over 2025-2026.

# Key Contracts

- **July 2026 — undisclosed European project**: Aker Solutions secured a
  new "substantial" contract (per its own classification, NOK 2.5-4 billion,
  roughly EUR 212-339 million) to deliver an HVDC substructure under an EPC
  scope, booked as Q2 2026 order intake in its Renewables and Field
  Development segment. Customer, project location, and delivery schedule
  were undisclosed as of the announcement.
- **East Anglia Three** (UK) — HVDC contract; offshore substation topside
  installed by the heavy-lift vessel Sleipnir (Oct 2025).
- **Norfolk Vanguard West and East** (UK, RWE) — HVDC contracts; full
  notice to proceed received (with Siemens Energy consortium) Jan 2025.
- **BalWin1** (Germany, 2 GW) — contract secured Feb 2025.
- **BalWin2** (Germany, 2 GW) — steel substructure for the offshore grid
  connection, secured June 2025.

# Skyborn Renewables

Offshore wind developer, a portfolio company of Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP).

## Key Projects

- [Nordergründe Offshore Wind Farm](/projects/nordergrunde.md) (Germany) — became sole
  owner in June 2026 after acquiring the remaining 70% stake.
- Revolution Wind (US, 704 MW) and South Fork Wind (US, 132 MW, operating) — joint
  ventures with [Ørsted](/companies/orsted.md), following Eversource's 2024 exit from
  US offshore wind.

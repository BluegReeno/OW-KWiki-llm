# Overview

Kent is an engineering and technical consulting firm offering independent
technical consulting and Owner's Engineer services across complex energy
projects, including offshore wind. In July 2026, Kent's Scotland team was
appointed Owner's Engineer for the [Buchan Offshore Wind Project](/projects/buchan-floating-wind.md),
a near-1 GW floating wind development off Aberdeenshire, Scotland, providing
integrated technical and delivery expertise to help derisk the project.

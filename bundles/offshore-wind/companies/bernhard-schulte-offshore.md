# Bernhard Schulte Offshore (BSO)

Bernhard Schulte Offshore (BSO), part of the Hamburg-based Bernhard Schulte
shipping group, operates a fleet of offshore wind service vessels under the
**WINDEA** brand, chartered out for turbine commissioning, walk-to-work, and
operations & maintenance support.

## CSOV Newbuild Series

Between mid-2025 and July 2026, BSO took delivery of a series of four
Commissioning Service Operation Vessels (CSOVs), all built to Ulstein
designs at Ulstein Verft in Norway:

* **Windea Clausius** - delivered 5 September 2025.
* **Windea Curie** - delivered June 2025.
* **Windea Carnot** - third in the series.
* **Windea Clarke** - delivered 2 July 2026, the fourth and final vessel in
  the series; entered an immediate charter in the UK North Sea.

Windea Clarke is the seventh Ulstein-designed offshore wind service vessel
in BSO's fleet overall.

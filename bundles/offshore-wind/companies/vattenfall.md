# Role

Vattenfall is a Swedish state-owned multinational utility and one of
Europe's established offshore wind developers, with projects across the
UK, the Netherlands, Denmark, and Germany. In Germany, Vattenfall is
developing the 1.6 GW Nordlicht cluster (Nordlicht I and II) in the North
Sea, for which it took final investment decision in January 2026;
construction began offshore in July 2026 with the first monopile
installed by contractor [DEME Offshore](/companies/deme-offshore.md).

# Key Projects

- [Nordlicht Offshore Wind Farms](/projects/nordlicht.md) — 1.6 GW cluster
  (980 MW Nordlicht I + 630 MW Nordlicht II) in the German North Sea,
  FID January 2026, offshore construction started July 2026, expected
  online 2028.

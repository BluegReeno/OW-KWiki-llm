# Overview

LS Cable & System is a South Korean manufacturer of power and communication
cables. LS GreenLink is its US subsidiary, set up to manufacture subsea and
underground high-voltage cables domestically for the American market,
including [HVDC](/technology/hvdc-grid-connection.md) export cables used to
connect offshore wind farms to shore.

# US Subsea Cable Factory

In July 2026, construction was progressing on LS GreenLink's US subsea cable
factory. The plant is being built around what is described as the world's
largest vertical continuous vulcanization (VCV) tower, a key piece of
equipment used to manufacture high-voltage cable cores. The facility is
intended to supply subsea and underground cables for the US offshore wind and
grid-interconnection market, reducing reliance on cable imports.

# Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP)

Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP) is a Danish fund manager and offshore
wind developer/investor, backing projects across Europe, the US, and
Asia-Pacific. It is a partner in [Vineyard Wind 1](/projects/vineyard-wind-1.md)
in the US (with Avangrid).

## South Korea

In July 2026, South Korea's government selected five offshore wind projects
totalling roughly 1,786 MW in its offshore wind auction. CIP secured 1 GW of
this capacity across two of its projects, including one reported as Haesong.

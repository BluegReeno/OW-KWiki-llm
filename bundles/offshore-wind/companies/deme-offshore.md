# Role

DEME Offshore is the offshore energy division of DEME Group, a Belgian marine
engineering and dredging group headquartered in Zwijndrecht, Belgium. DEME
Offshore provides transport & installation (T&I) and EPCI (engineering,
procurement, construction, installation) services for offshore wind farms:
monopile, jacket, and XL-monopile foundations, inter-array and export cables,
offshore substations, and scour protection. It works as a contractor for
developers such as [Ørsted](/companies/orsted.md), [RWE](/companies/rwe-offshore-wind.md),
[Equinor](/companies/equinor.md), and [Iberdrola](/companies/iberdrola.md)
rather than developing projects itself.

DEME Group reported record 2025 turnover of €4.2 billion and an order book of
€7.4 billion, with its dredging and offshore divisions both contributing
strongly. It operates a fleet of over 100 specialised vessels and employs
close to 6,000 people, with regional joint ventures including Japan Offshore
Marine (JOM) and CDWE (Taiwan) extending its installation capacity in Asia.

# Fleet / Capabilities

- **Orion** — DP3 offshore installation vessel, used for large monopile and
  turbine installation campaigns.
- **Innovation** — installation vessel used for early XL-monopile work
  (e.g., Saint-Nazaire).
- **Norse Wind** and **Norse Energi** — newly delivered next-generation
  turbine installation vessels (WTIVs), each equipped with a 3,200-tonne
  crane, able to install turbines with rotor diameters over 300 m and
  XXL monopiles up to 3,000 tonnes in water depths up to 70 m; both entered
  service in 2026, starting on Iberdrola's Windanker and RWE's Nordseecluster
  projects.
- **Living Stone** and **Viking Neptun** — cable installation vessels.
- Regional joint ventures: Japan Offshore Marine (JOM) in Japan; CDWE in
  Taiwan (active since 2019).

# Key Projects

- [Saint-Nazaire](/projects/saint-nazaire.md) — installed all 80 monopile
  foundations and the offshore substation for France's first commercial
  offshore wind farm, using the vessel Innovation.
- [Hornsea 2](/projects/hornsea-2.md) — T&I contract (value over €200 million)
  for 165 monopile foundations and scour protection, using the vessel Orion.
- [Vineyard Wind 1](/projects/vineyard-wind-1.md) — foundation installation
  contractor: monopiles, transition pieces, offshore substation, and scour
  protection (contract value ~€150–300 million).
- [Empire Wind 1](/projects/empire-wind-1.md) — inter-array cable transport
  and installation (jointly with Empire Wind 2), over 350 km of cable, using
  the vessels Living Stone and Viking Neptun; developer
  [Equinor](/companies/equinor.md) with bp.
- [Changhua](/projects/changhua.md) — contributed to Taiwan's Greater
  Changhua offshore wind development and related projects (Zhong Neng,
  Changfang Xidao, Hai Long, Formosa 4) through the CDWE joint venture.

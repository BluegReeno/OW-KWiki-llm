# Policy

* [EU Offshore Renewable Energy Strategy](eu-offshore-wind-strategy.md) - European Commission strategy setting EU-wide offshore wind targets of 60 GW by 2030 and 300 GW by 2050
* [UK Contracts for Difference (CfD) Scheme](uk-contracts-for-difference-scheme.md) - The UK's auction mechanism guaranteeing renewable generators a strike price, underpinning successive offshore wind allocation rounds
* [French Programmation Pluriannuelle de l'Énergie (PPE)](french-energy-policy-ppe.md) - France's multi-year energy programming law setting the national offshore wind capacity trajectory and tender calendar
* [US BOEM Offshore Wind Leasing Process](us-boem-leasing-process.md) - The federal process run by the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management for leasing and permitting offshore wind on the Outer Continental Shelf
* [French Offshore Wind Permitting Process](french-permitting-eolien-en-mer.md) - The French administrative process for authorizing offshore wind farms, from public debate through DUP and the single environmental authorization

# Technology

* [Floating Offshore Wind](floating-wind.md) - Turbines on buoyant moored platforms, unlocking deep-water sites beyond fixed-bottom reach.
* [Fixed-Bottom Foundations](fixed-bottom-foundations.md) - Monopile, jacket, and gravity-based structures supporting most installed offshore wind capacity.
* [HVDC Grid Connection](hvdc-grid-connection.md) - High-voltage DC transmission via offshore converter platforms for large or distant wind farms.
* [Power-to-X (Offshore Green Hydrogen)](power-to-x.md) - Converting offshore wind electricity directly into hydrogen or other energy carriers at sea.
* [Offshore Substations](offshore-substations.md) - Platforms collecting and stepping up turbine power before export to shore.
* [Turbine Scaling](turbine-scaling.md) - Growth in turbine capacity, rotor diameter, and hub height, and its economic impact.

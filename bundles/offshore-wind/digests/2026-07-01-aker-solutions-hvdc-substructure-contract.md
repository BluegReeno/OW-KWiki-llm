# Summary

Aker Solutions announced on 30 June 2026 that it secured a "substantial"
contract — by its own classification, NOK 2.5-4 billion (~EUR 212-339
million) — to deliver an HVDC substructure under an EPC scope for an
undisclosed offshore wind project in Europe. The award will be booked as
order intake in Q2 2026 within the company's Renewables and Field
Development segment. Customer, project location, and delivery schedule
were not disclosed.

The contract adds to a run of offshore wind HVDC/grid-connection work
Aker Solutions has secured over 2025-2026, including East Anglia Three,
Norfolk Vanguard West/East, and Germany's BalWin1 and BalWin2 grid
connections. Created a new company page, [Aker Solutions](/companies/aker-solutions.md),
and cross-linked it from [HVDC Grid Connection](/technology/hvdc-grid-connection.md).

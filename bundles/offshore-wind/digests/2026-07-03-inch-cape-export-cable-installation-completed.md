# Export Cable Installation Completed at Inch Cape Offshore Wind Farm

The second export cable installation campaign for the
[Inch Cape Offshore Wind Farm](/projects/inch-cape.md) (Scotland, 1.1 GW,
[ESB](/companies/esb.md)/Red Rock Renewables) has been completed, cable
supplier Orient Cable (NBO) announced. The export cables were installed by
Enshore Subsea, which laid the first of the two 85 km power lines in October
2025. NBO is now moving on to offshore jointing and onshore/offshore platform
jointing and termination, with all interface scope on track for completion by
end of 2026. The export cable will connect the Inch Cape offshore substation
to a new onshore substation at Cockenzie, East Lothian. First power is
expected late 2026.

# Skyborn Becomes Sole Owner of Nordergründe Offshore Wind Farm

Skyborn Renewables has completed the acquisition of a 70% stake in the
[Nordergründe Offshore Wind Farm](/projects/nordergrunde.md), making it the sole
owner of the German project. This adds a new fully-owned asset to
[Skyborn Renewables](/companies/skyborn.md)' portfolio, alongside its US joint
ventures with [Ørsted](/companies/orsted.md) (Revolution Wind, South Fork Wind).

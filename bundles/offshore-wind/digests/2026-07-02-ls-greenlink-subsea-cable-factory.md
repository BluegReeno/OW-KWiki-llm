# Summary

[LS GreenLink](/companies/ls-cable-system.md), the US subsidiary of South
Korean manufacturer LS Cable & System, has started construction work on a
subsea power cable factory in the US, built around what is described as the
world's largest vertical continuous vulcanization (VCV) tower. The plant is
intended to supply domestically manufactured subsea and underground
high-voltage cables, including HVDC export cables, for the US offshore wind
and grid-interconnection market.

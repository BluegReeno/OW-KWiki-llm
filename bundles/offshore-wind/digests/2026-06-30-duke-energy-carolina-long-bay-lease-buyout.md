# Summary

The US Department of the Interior (DOI) reached a settlement agreement with
Duke Energy under which Duke exits its Carolina Long Bay offshore wind lease
off North Carolina. This is the fourth offshore wind lease buyout deal
reached by the Trump administration, continuing the federal effort to unwind
offshore wind leases on the Outer Continental Shelf. See
[US BOEM Offshore Wind Leasing Process](/policy/us-boem-leasing-process.md)
for the wider policy context.

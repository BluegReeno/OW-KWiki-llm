# Gwynt Glas Submits EIA Scoping Report for 1.5 GW Celtic Sea Floating Wind Project

The developers of the proposed [Gwynt Glas](/projects/gwynt-glas.md) floating
wind farm — a joint venture between EDF Power Solutions and ESB, working with
DP Energy as exclusive development partner — have submitted an Environmental
Impact Assessment (EIA) Scoping Report to the UK Planning Inspectorate (PINS),
Natural Resources Wales, and the Marine Management Organisation. The
submission opens formal stakeholder engagement on the topics and
methodologies proposed for the EIA, ahead of future consent applications for
the 1.5 GW Celtic Sea project, secured via the Crown Estate's Round 5 floating
offshore wind leasing process. Public consultation is expected in autumn 2026.

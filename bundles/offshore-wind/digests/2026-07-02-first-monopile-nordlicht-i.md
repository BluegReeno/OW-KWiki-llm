# Summary

[DEME Offshore](/companies/deme-offshore.md) installed the first monopile
at the [Nordlicht I](/projects/nordlicht.md) offshore wind farm site in the
German North Sea on 1 July 2026, marking the start of offshore construction
on [Vattenfall](/companies/vattenfall.md)'s 1.6 GW Nordlicht cluster
(980 MW Nordlicht I + 630 MW Nordlicht II). DEME holds the transport &
installation contract for monopile foundations and transition pieces at
both wind farms; monopiles are manufactured by EEW Special Pipe
Constructions and transition pieces by CS Wind. Foundation installation at
Nordlicht II is due to start around a year later, with both wind farms
expected online in 2028.

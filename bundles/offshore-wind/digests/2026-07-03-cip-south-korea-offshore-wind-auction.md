# CIP Secures 1 GW Across Two Projects in South Korea's Offshore Wind Auction

South Korea's government selected five offshore wind projects with a
combined capacity of approximately 1,786 MW in its offshore wind auction.
[Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP)](/companies/copenhagen-infrastructure-partners.md)
secured 1 GW of the awarded capacity across two of its projects, including
one reported as Haesong, marking a new market entry for CIP tracked here as
the [South Korea Offshore Wind Auction (2026)](/tenders/south-korea-offshore-wind-auction-2026.md).

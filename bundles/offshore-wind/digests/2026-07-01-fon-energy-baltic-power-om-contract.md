# Føn Energy Services Secures Multi-Year O&M Contract for Baltic Power Offshore Wind Farm

Føn Energy Services Poland has signed a long-term, multi-million-euro agreement with the joint
venture behind [Baltic Power](/projects/baltic-power.md), Poland's first offshore wind farm
(~1,140 MW, ORLEN and Northland Power), to provide operations & maintenance (O&M) services.
This is the first dedicated concept page for the Baltic Power project in this bundle; it was
previously only referenced as a [Vestas](/companies/vestas.md) turbine order.

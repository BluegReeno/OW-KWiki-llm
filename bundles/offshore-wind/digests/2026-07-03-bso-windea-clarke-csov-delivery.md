# Bernhard Schulte Offshore Takes Delivery of Fourth CSOV in Newbuild Series

[Bernhard Schulte Offshore (BSO)](/companies/bernhard-schulte-offshore.md) has
taken delivery of Windea Clarke, the fourth and final Commissioning Service
Operation Vessel (CSOV) in a newbuild series built at Ulstein Verft in
Norway and delivered since mid-2025. Windea Clarke has entered an immediate
charter in the UK North Sea; it is the seventh Ulstein-designed offshore
wind service vessel in BSO's fleet.

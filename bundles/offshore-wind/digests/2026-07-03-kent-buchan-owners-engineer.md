# Summary

Buchan Offshore Wind — a joint venture between BayWa r.e., BW Ideol and
Elicio developing a near-1 GW floating wind farm off Fraserburgh,
Aberdeenshire (a [ScotWind](/tenders/scotwind-leasing-round.md) award) —
has appointed **Kent** as Owner's Engineer, to provide integrated technical
and delivery expertise as the project advances.

New concept pages: [Buchan Offshore Wind Project](/projects/buchan-floating-wind.md),
[Kent](/companies/kent.md).

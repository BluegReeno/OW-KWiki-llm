# JERA Nex BP Takes Full Ownership of Northwester 2, Raises Stake in Nobelwind

[JERA Nex BP](/companies/jera-nex-bp.md) has completed the acquisition of [Sumitomo Corporation](#)'s remaining stakes in two Belgian offshore wind farms: the full 30% stake in [Northwester 2](/projects/northwester-2.md), taking it to 100% ownership, and a 39.02% stake in [Nobelwind](/projects/nobelwind.md), raising its holding there to 80.1%. Both projects are operating wind farms in the Belgian North Sea developed and operated by JERA Nex BP from its Ostend hub.

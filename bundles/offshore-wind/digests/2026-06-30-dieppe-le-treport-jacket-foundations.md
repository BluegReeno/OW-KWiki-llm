# Summary

[DEME Offshore](/companies/deme-offshore.md) has completed installation of
the jacket foundations at the [Dieppe-Le Tréport offshore wind
farm](/projects/dieppe-le-treport.md), off the coast of Normandy, France.
The article's categorization also associates Jan De Nul, Navantia
Seanergies, Ocean Winds, and Windar Renovables with the project. Only a
short excerpt of the source article was available at ingestion time; see
the citation below for the full article.

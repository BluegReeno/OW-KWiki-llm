# Lithuania's First Offshore Wind Tender (2023)

In 2023, Lithuania held its first offshore wind auction, awarding development rights for a 700 MW
site in the Lithuanian sector of the Baltic Sea. A joint venture between
[Ignitis Renewables](/companies/ignitis-renewables.md) and Ocean Winds won the tender, and the
site was subsequently developed as the [Curonian Nord](/projects/curonian-nord.md) offshore wind
farm.

In 2025, Ignitis and Ocean Winds signed a sale and purchase agreement under which Ignitis acquired
full ownership of the Curonian Nord project, becoming sole owner.

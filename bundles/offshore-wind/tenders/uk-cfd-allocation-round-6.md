# Overview

UK Contracts for Difference (CfD) Allocation Round 6 (AR6) is administered
under the mechanism described in
[UK Contracts for Difference scheme](/policy/uk-contracts-for-difference-scheme.md) —
this page covers only what is specific to this round, not the CfD scheme
itself.

**Correction on which round had zero offshore wind bids:** it was
**Allocation Round 5 (AR5, results published 8 September 2023)**, not AR6,
that received no bids at all for offshore wind — the Administrative Strike
Price cap of £44/MWh fell well short of what was commercially viable amid
sharp increases in steel and turbine costs. AR6 was the government's
response: it raised the offshore wind budget to £1.555 billion (a 50%+
increase on the original March 2024 budget, and roughly 7x AR5's budget)
and increased the Administrative Strike Price, which successfully drew
bids back. AR6 results, announced 3 September 2024, awarded **nearly
5.3 GW of offshore wind capacity** (including floating wind) — a strong
rebound, though short of AR4's record 7.0 GW — out of 9.6 GW awarded
across all technologies. Confirmed winners include the 400 MW (up to
560 MW installed) Green Volt floating wind project off Aberdeenshire, a
266 MW top-up award for ESB/Red Rock's Inch Cape (on top of its AR4
award), and a 73.5 MW award to Ocean Winds (EDP Renewables/ENGIE) for
Moray West.

# Key Facts

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Organizer | DESNZ (Department for Energy Security and Net Zero) sets budget/parameters; NESO (National Energy System Operator), as Electricity Market Reform Delivery Body, administers the auction |
| Market | United Kingdom, all technologies (offshore wind is Pot 3) |
| Results announced | 3 September 2024 |
| Offshore wind capacity awarded | ~5.3 GW (fixed-bottom and floating combined) |
| Offshore wind budget | £1.555 billion (raised from original AR6 budget, ~7x AR5's offshore wind budget) |
| Notable winners | Green Volt (floating, ~400–560 MW, Aberdeenshire); Inch Cape (266 MW top-up); Moray West (73.5 MW, Ocean Winds) |
| Total capacity, all technologies | 9.6 GW |
| Status | Concluded — contracts awarded; preceded [UK CfD Allocation Round 7](/tenders/uk-cfd-allocation-round-7.md) |

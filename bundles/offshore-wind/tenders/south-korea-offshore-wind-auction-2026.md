# South Korea Offshore Wind Auction (2026)

In July 2026, South Korea selected five offshore wind projects with a
combined capacity of roughly 1,786 MW in its national offshore wind auction.

[Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP)](/companies/copenhagen-infrastructure-partners.md)
secured 1 GW of the awarded capacity across two of its projects, including
one reported as Haesong. Winners and details for the remaining projects were
not covered in the source excerpt.

# Northwester 2 Offshore Wind Farm

219 MW offshore wind farm located 51 km off the Belgian coast in the North Sea, comprising 23 Vestas 9.5 MW turbines. Operational since 2020. Developed, built, and operated by [JERA Nex BP](/companies/jera-nex-bp.md), which took full ownership in July 2026 by acquiring Sumitomo Corporation's remaining 30% stake.

The wind farm has a long-term power purchase agreement (PPA) signed in 2019 under which RWE Supply & Trading procures its full electricity output and resells it to industrial and municipal customers, including ASML.

## Facts
- **Capacity**: 219 MW
- **Location**: Belgian North Sea, 51 km offshore
- **Turbines**: 23 x Vestas 9.5 MW
- **Operational since**: 2020
- **Owner**: JERA Nex BP (100%, since July 2026; previously 70% JERA Nex BP / 30% Sumitomo Corporation)
- **Offtake**: Long-term PPA with RWE Supply & Trading (since 2019), including offtake to ASML

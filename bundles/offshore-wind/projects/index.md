# Projects

* [Dogger Bank Wind Farm](dogger-bank.md) - UK, North Sea, 3.6 GW across Phases A/B/C, the world's largest offshore wind farm, SSE Renewables/Equinor/Vårgrønn.
* [Hornsea 2](hornsea-2.md) - UK, North Sea, 1,320 MW, Ørsted, fully operational since 2022.
* [Vineyard Wind 1](vineyard-wind-1.md) - US, Massachusetts, 806 MW, first major commercial-scale US offshore wind farm, Avangrid/CIP.
* [Empire Wind 1](empire-wind-1.md) - US, New York, 810 MW, Equinor, under construction, first power expected late 2026.
* [Saint-Brieuc](saint-brieuc.md) - France, Brittany, 496 MW, Ailes Marines/Iberdrola, fully commissioned May 2024.
* [Saint-Nazaire](saint-nazaire.md) - France, Loire-Atlantique, 480 MW, EDF Renouvelables/EIH, first offshore wind farm commissioned in France.
* [Changhua Offshore Wind Farms (Greater Changhua)](changhua.md) - Taiwan, 1,820 MW across four phases, Ørsted with Taiwanese partners.
* [Nordlicht Offshore Wind Farms (Nordlicht I & II)](nordlicht.md) - Germany, North Sea, 1.6 GW (980 MW + 630 MW), Vattenfall, offshore construction started July 2026, online 2028.
* [Nordergründe Offshore Wind Farm](nordergrunde.md) - Germany, operating offshore wind farm, now wholly owned by Skyborn Renewables after it acquired the remaining 70% stake.
* [Dieppe-Le Tréport Offshore Wind Farm](dieppe-le-treport.md) - France, Normandy, 496 MW, Engie-led consortium/Ocean Winds, all jacket foundations installed June 2026 by DEME Offshore.
* [Baltic Power Offshore Wind Farm](baltic-power.md) - Poland, Baltic Sea, ~1,140 MW, ORLEN/Northland Power JV, 76 Vestas V236-15.0 MW turbines; O&M contract awarded to Føn Energy Services July 2026.
* [Buchan Offshore Wind Project](buchan-floating-wind.md) - Scotland, ~1 GW floating wind farm off Aberdeenshire (ScotWind), BayWa r.e./BW Ideol/Elicio JV; Kent appointed Owner's Engineer July 2026.
* [Gwynt Glas Floating Wind Project](gwynt-glas.md) - Wales, Celtic Sea, up to 1.5 GW floating wind farm, EDF Power Solutions/ESB JV with DP Energy; EIA Scoping Report submitted July 2026, Crown Estate Round 5 lease.
* [Inch Cape Offshore Wind Farm](inch-cape.md) - Scotland, North Sea, 1.1 GW, ESB/Red Rock Renewables JV, 72 Vestas V236-15.0 MW turbines; second export cable installation campaign completed July 2026, first power expected late 2026.
* [Northwester 2 Offshore Wind Farm](northwester-2.md) - Belgium, North Sea, 219 MW, operating since 2020, now wholly owned by JERA Nex BP after it bought out Sumitomo Corporation's 30% stake, July 2026.
* [Nobelwind Offshore Wind Farm](nobelwind.md) - Belgium, North Sea, 165 MW, operating since 2017 (Belwind phase 2), JERA Nex BP raised its stake to 80.1% by acquiring Sumitomo Corporation's 39.02% interest, July 2026.
* [Curonian Nord Offshore Wind Farm](curonian-nord.md) - Lithuania, Baltic Sea, 700 MW, Lithuania's first offshore wind project, developed by Ignitis Renewables; received positive EIA decision July 2026, construction permit expected 2027.

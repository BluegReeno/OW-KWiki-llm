# Baltic Power Offshore Wind Farm

Baltic Power is a ~1,140 MW offshore wind farm in the Polish Baltic Sea, Poland's first offshore
wind project, developed as a joint venture between ORLEN and Northland Power. It is being fitted
with 76 [Vestas](/companies/vestas.md) V236-15.0 MW turbines; the first turbine was installed in
2025, with commissioning expected in 2026.

In July 2026, the Baltic Power joint venture awarded [Føn Energy Services](/companies/fon-energy-services.md)
(via its Polish arm) a long-term, multi-million-euro operations & maintenance (O&M) contract for
the wind farm.

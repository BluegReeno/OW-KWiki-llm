# Overview

Buchan Offshore Wind is a floating offshore wind project located roughly
75 km north-east of Fraserburgh, Aberdeenshire, Scotland, targeting a
capacity of up to ~1 GW (reported as 960 MW-1 GW across sources) using up
to 70 floating turbines. The site was secured through the
[ScotWind leasing round](/tenders/scotwind-leasing-round.md), Crown Estate
Scotland's seabed leasing process.

The project is being developed by a joint venture of **BayWa r.e.**,
**BW Ideol** (floating foundation specialist) and **Elicio**. In July 2026,
the JV appointed **Kent**, an engineering and technical-consulting firm, as
Owner's Engineer, tasked with providing integrated technical and delivery
expertise to derisk the project as it advances toward consenting and FID.

# Nobelwind Offshore Wind Farm

165 MW offshore wind farm located 47 km off the Belgian coast, commissioned in 2017 as a second phase of the Belwind offshore wind farm. It features 50 Vestas V112-3.3 MW turbines. Developed, built, and operated by [JERA Nex BP](/companies/jera-nex-bp.md), which raised its stake to 80.1% in July 2026 by acquiring Sumitomo Corporation's 39.02% interest.

In 2024, Nobelwind was equipped with a vessel charging station system for crew transfer vessels (CTVs, up to 2 MW) and service operation vessels (SOVs, up to 8 MW).

## Facts
- **Capacity**: 165 MW
- **Location**: Belgian North Sea, 47 km offshore
- **Turbines**: 50 x Vestas V112-3.3 MW
- **Commissioned**: 2017 (Belwind phase 2)
- **Owner**: JERA Nex BP (80.1%, since July 2026; previously ~41% JERA Nex BP / 39.02% Sumitomo Corporation)
